Test your AI before real people use it

Government agencies deploy AI that answers questions from the public. Evergreen lets policy experts verify those answers are safe, accurate, and helpful — before launch.

Get started →

How it works

  1. Write test cases

    Pick a template or start from scratch. Write the questions real people ask and define what a correct answer looks like.

  2. Run the evaluation

    Evergreen sends each question to your AI and grades every response automatically across five quality dimensions.

  3. Read the report

    Get a clear pass/fail for every test case, a deployment recommendation, and an artifact you can share with leadership.

What you get

  • Evidence for decision-makers. A concrete report showing whether your AI is ready to deploy — not a vendor’s promise, your own test results.
  • Catch problems before the public does. Safety failures, wrong facts, confusing language — surface them in a test, not a news story.
  • Re-run any time something changes. Model update, prompt revision, new scope — re-evaluate in minutes, not weeks.
  • Your data stays on your infrastructure. Open source. No external storage. No vendor lock-in.
